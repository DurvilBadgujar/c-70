Prodigy Public School (Wagholi Campus) commenced its first academic session on 12th June 2006. It is a co-educational English medium day school which is affiliated to the CBSE Curriculum (Central Board of Secondary Education) from Nursery to Class XII. The sprawling school campus has a lush green pollution free environment which blends seamlessly in the outskirts of the megalopolis. With an infrastructure that supports excellence in all areas, Prodigy Public School provides a wide array of facilities in Academics, Sports, IT, and Habitat. We provide a totally balanced environment focused on shaping children into leaders of tomorrow by offering comprehensive education through a developmental approach.
